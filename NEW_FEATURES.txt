2. LIVES SYSTEM
   - Start with 3 lives per game
   - Lose a life when health reaches 0
   - Game over after losing all 3 lives
   - Lives display on HUD

3. POWER-UP SYSTEM
   - Shield Power-up (Green S icon)
     * Increases max health by 30
     * Restores health to full
     * Randomly drops from defeated enemies
   
   - Rapid Fire Power-up (Orange RF icon)
     * Increases shooting speed
     * Active for 8 seconds
     * Allows rapid enemy elimination
     * Visual indicator on HUD

4. COMBO MULTIPLIER SYSTEM
   - Combo increases by 0.1x for each enemy destroyed
   - Maximum combo: 5x
   - Resets after 5 seconds without a kill
   - Score multiplied by combo (e.g., 100 points at 2x = 200 points)
   - Displayed on HUD in real-time

HOW TO USE NEW FEATURES

SELECTING DIFFICULTY
1. Start the game
2. Choose difficulty from dropdown menu
3. Click "START GAME"
4. Game adjusts enemies, health, and spawn rates

USING POWER-UPS
1. Defeat enemies to randomly obtain power-ups
2. Walk over power-ups to collect them
3. Shield: Heals and increases max health
4. Rapid Fire: Makes you shoot faster for 8 seconds
5. Watch HUD for active power-up indicators

BUILDING COMBOS
1. Kill enemies consecutively without dying
2. Each kill adds 0.1x to your combo
3. Kill quickly to build higher combos (max 5x)
4. Score multiplies by your combo (2x combo = 2x points)
5. Combo resets after 5 seconds of no kills
